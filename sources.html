📖 Book Recommendations

The China Study – T. Colin Campbell (2005)
Comprehensive look at nutrition and plant-based diets.
Eating Animals – Jonathan Safran Foer (2009)
Explores the ethical and environmental issues of animal agriculture.
How Not to Die – Michael Greger (2015)
Focuses on evidence-based nutrition for preventing diseases.

🎬 Movie / Documentary Recommendations

Forks Over Knives (2011)
Explores the health benefits of a whole-food, plant-based diet.
Seaspiracy (2021)
Investigates the environmental impact of fishing industries.
Dominion (2018)
Exposes animal agriculture practices worldwide.
